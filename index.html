Air Quality Control and Hazardous Substance Detection for Any Space

Our air quality control and hazardous substance detection services help ensure health and comfort in residential and commercial spaces. We detect gas leaks and identify contaminants, including mold, herbicides, pesticides, radon, asbestos, and formaldehydes.

We also utilize innovative detection technologies, including rapid chromatography with on-site reporting of test results. Additionally, we monitor environmental impacts such as pollution from emissions and leaks. For high-risk areas, we offer services for detecting airborne viruses.

Our Testing Formats

One-Time Visit

Our specialist will conduct all necessary experiments at your site, and you will receive a detailed report with the testing results.

Long-Term Testing

We will install equipment at your site, and you will perform tests at scheduled intervals. You will receive interim reports, as well as a comprehensive report at the end of the entire testing period.

Multiple Visits by Subscription

We can conduct regular studies at your site, which is essential when it's necessary to track the temporal dynamics of air quality changes. You will receive both interim reports and updated progress reports, where current test data will be compared to the results of previous studies.

Our Equipment

At Eco Air Lab, we use state-of-the-art, high-precision equipment to ensure the highest quality in our air quality testing. Our inventory includes advanced chromatographs, sensitive analytical instruments, and specialized detectors. We also take pride in our proprietary technologies, developed in-house, that allow us to deliver accurate and reliable results. All of our equipment is carefully selected for its ability to detect even the most subtle changes in air quality and environmental conditions.

  • Chromatographs
  • Real-Time Detection Sensors
  • Radon Detectors

At Eco Air Lab, we use high-precision chromatographs that play a key role in our air quality testing. These sophisticated instruments allow us to separate and analyze gases and compounds present in the air with unparalleled accuracy. By providing detailed breakdowns of the chemical composition in the air, our chromatographs help us detect even trace amounts of harmful substances, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of the environment’s air quality.

Our real-time detection sensors are designed to continuously monitor air quality and environmental conditions. These advanced devices provide instant feedback on various parameters such as particulate matter, volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and other airborne pollutants. This allows us to offer immediate insights into air quality, ensuring that potential hazards are identified as soon as they arise, enabling timely action and mitigation.

Radon is a naturally occurring radioactive gas that can be harmful to health if present in high concentrations. Our radon detectors are equipped with the latest technology to accurately measure radon levels in indoor environments. With these detectors, we can identify areas with elevated radon concentrations and provide recommendations for improving air safety. Early detection of radon exposure is crucial, as it can prevent long-term health risks associated with this dangerous gas.
