Technology Applications

Our air quality analysis and monitoring services are designed to ensure a safe and healthy environment in any space. We offer both one-time assessments to detect pollutants and long-term solutions for continuous air and ventilation monitoring.

Indoor Health and Safety

Our Indoor Health and Safety Assessment employs advanced methods and portable gas chromatography to evaluate indoor VOCs, including the most common and dangerous pollutants such as formaldehyde, benzene, toluene, xylene, ethylene glycol, acetone, methylene chloride, perchloroethylene, styrene, and trichloroethylene.

Learn More
Indoor Well-Being

Our Indoor Well-Being Assessment employs advanced methods and a multi-sensory system to evaluate air quality, light levels, noise, and bad odors. We offer insights into short and long-term health, sleep quality, cognitive function, and building health. This comprehensive analysis helps create a healthier, more comfortable living and working environment.

Learn More
Mold Inspection

Our advanced Mold Detection Service utilizes Mold-Specific Volatile Organic Compound (MSVOC) Detectors. These portable devices are designed to detect the unique gaseous compounds emitted by mold, allowing for rapid on-site analysis. This technology enables quick and accurate identification of mold-contaminated areas, ensuring timely and effective remediation.

Learn More
Radon Detection

Ensure the safety of your indoor environment with our comprehensive Radon Testing Service. Utilizing state-of-the-art, certified methods and detectors, we provide accurate and reliable testing to identify radon levels in your property. Our advanced techniques cover multiple areas for thorough on-site analysis, ensuring no hidden radon pockets are missed.

Drug Residue Detection

Ensure a safe and compliant indoor environment with our Drug Residue Detection Service. Using advanced methods, we provide accurate and reliable testing to identify drug residues in indoor air. Our comprehensive on-site analysis covers multiple areas, ensuring thorough detection and assessment.
